Share experiences

Share things with friends, have one person pay, and note it in the app. Next time someone else pays. You always know who is currently in the red.

Share expenses

You can enter any constellation of participants for an expense. And have multiple people pay arbitrary parts of it. It’s easy and flexible.

Mobile & offline

Grouptabs is an offline-capable, installable web app and allows collaborating without user accounts. It works great on mobile devices!
